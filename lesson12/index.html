Our Purpose

We are Udi Commerce and our purpose is to help small local business to advertise their products and services through marketing and network with other members of the chamber. We seek to strengthen local business in Uberlandia, helping them reach out to more people and grow, and as result of their growth, become a source of employment for citizens of city.
