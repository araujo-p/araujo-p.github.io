Uberlandia

Uberlandia is a city situated on the Triangulo Mineiro (Miner Triangle). Its first sign of habitants started at XIX century, which were a group of "bandeirantes" seeking for the Sertao da Faria Podre. It was declared an official town on August 31 1888.
