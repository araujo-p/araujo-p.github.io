Target Audience

The target audience are the travelers who wants to know how the weather will be like when they travel to a specific location so they can pack up accordingly. We also target those families who likes to live providently, the ones who wants to know if their is a storm approching or any weather issues that may keep them from getting out of their houses and that will require food storage.
