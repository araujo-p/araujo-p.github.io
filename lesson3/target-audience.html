Site Plan | Weather Site Project

Patricia Araujo | WDD230-05

Target Audience

  • Who: people who are married or single, who likes to travel or like to stay safe when there is brutal change in the climate or alerts about a possible catastrophe around the place where they live.
  • Age: from 18 to 100 years.
  • Technology: simple and easy to use because our target audience may be people who doesn’t have much abilities with technology.
  • Income: it will vary from $18,000 annually or above because we also target young single adults and families.
  • Motivation: provide accurate information about the weather to help people keep safe and also travel more prepared to the kind of weather they will face it.
